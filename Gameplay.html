Gameplay

Get familiar with our gameplay !

Robin Hood: New Generation is a platform game that puts players in control of a character named Robin, who embarks on a mission to steal from houses and collect money for charity. The ultimate goal is to complete each level without getting caught by the police. The game features both single-player and multiplayer modes, each offering unique challenges and gameplay options. Single-Player Mode: In the single-player mode, players navigate Robin through various levels filled with houses to rob. The primary objective is to collect as much money as possible from each house without getting apprehended. The houses will be filled with valuable items, treasure chests, and hidden compartments where money is stashed. As Robin progresses through the levels, he will face different obstacles and challenges that slow down his progress. These obstacles can include security systems, guard dogs, locked doors, and traps. Players must utilize Robin's skills and abilities to overcome these challenges, such as climbing walls and sneaking past guards. At the end of each level, the collected money is tallied up, and players have the option to donate it to charity. The amount of money donated will affect the overall score and player progression. Multiplayer Mode: The multiplayer mode expands on the single-player experience and offers an exciting competitive and cooperative gameplay experience. Players can team up with friends or compete against each other in various levels and game modes. New Enemies: The multiplayer mode introduces new enemies and rival thieves who will try to steal money from players. Robin and other players must outsmart and outmaneuver these opponents to secure the most money for their charities. Artificial Intelligence: The game will incorporate AI-controlled characters and they contribute to the overall story progression and provide additional gameplay challenges. Overall, Robin Hood: New Generation offers a dynamic and thrilling platform game experience, combining elements of stealth and strategy. Players must navigate through various obstacles, outsmart enemies, and complete levels while collecting money for charity. With its single-player and multiplayer story mode, the game provides a range of gameplay options and challenges to keep players engaged and entertained.
